The 22nd "CIETAC Cup" will kick off on November 29, 2024. It has attracted more than 1,000 participants from over 100 law schools across China and overseas regions, including the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, Russia, Egypt, Vietnam, Kazakhstan, as well as China’s Hong Kong and Macao.

[图片]
About the CIETAC Cup

The CIETAC Cup is an annual professional arbitration competition for law schools hosted by the China International Economic and Trade Arbitration Commission (CIETAC). Founded in 2000, the competition has successfully held 21 sessions to date, attracting over 10,000 students from more than 200 renowned higher education institutions.

With the mission of fostering international arbitration legal talents in China and across the globe, the CIETAC Cup promotes the arbitration system and enhances the legal literacy and practical arbitration capabilities of young students at home and abroad. It adopts internationally advanced moot arbitration problems and simulated courtroom proceedings in tandem with global practices. Serving as the domestic selection tournament for the Willem C. Vis International Commercial Arbitration Moot (Vis Moot), the CIETAC Cup is also recognized by the Vis Moot as the world’s first pre-moot competition. It applies the same case materials and arbitration rules as the Vis Moot, with English as the official competition language. This year’s CIETAC Cup has also received strong support from prestigious organizations and institutions including the Vis Moot, MAA, ICC, HKIAC, SIAC, VMAA, AIAC and KCAB.
